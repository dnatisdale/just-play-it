Playlist Guide

Master your music library offline.

Building a playlist in JUST PLAY IT. is simple and tab-based. Your "Current Queue" in the sidebar is your active workspace.

1. Import Your Music

Add files to your device library.

  • Library Tab: Tap the Library tab at the bottom.
  • Floating Action Button: Tap the large red + button in the bottom right corner.
  • Choose Files: Select Pick Audio Files or Pick Folder to import tracks into the app.

2. Build Your Queue

Select tracks to play now or later.

  • Select Tracks: In the Library tab, tap the circular selection buttons on the right side of the tracks you want.
  • Add to Queue: Tap the Add to Playlist button that appears at the bottom of the list.
  • View Queue: Open the Sidebar (hamburger menu) to see your current queue.

3. Organize and Save

Save your groupings for later.

  • Playlists Tab: Tap the Playlists tab at the bottom.
  • Create: Enter a name in the "New playlist name..." box and tap CREATE.
  • Load: Tap any saved playlist card to load it into your current queue.
Pro Tip Use the Edit button in the sidebar queue to reorder tracks using drag handles or remove them quickly.
