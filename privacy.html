Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

1. Data We Do Not Collect

JUST PLAY IT. is designed with your absolute privacy in mind. We believe in keeping data strictly on your device.

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not transmit, upload, or sync your audio files, playlists, or listening history to our servers.
  • We do not require you to create an account or sign in.

2. Local Data Processing

All core functionality operates locally on your device:

  • Local Storage & IndexedDB: Any audio files you add, custom playlists you create, or preferences you save (like Dark Mode settings and Sleep Timers) are strictly stored within your web browser's local sandbox (IndexedDB and Local Storage).
  • When you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, this data is completely erased.

3. Third-Party Analytics

To improve the performance and reliability of the application, we may use standard anonymous telemetry (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) measuring generalized app performance (e.g., crash reporting, installation counts, and page views). This data is aggregated and contains no personally identifiable information (PII).

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top. Because we don't collect contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please reach out to us on our support page at Ko-fi.com/justplayit.
